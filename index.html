FABIAN GRÖNING




*1992 in Berlin. Lives and works in Paris and Arles.
Fabian Gröning is currently working as a project manager for strategic initiatives at LUMA Arles. He completed internships as a curatorial assistant at KW Institute of Contemporary Art KUNST-WERKE Berlin and Centre Pompidou, before working as a mediator at Art Basel and Centre Pompidou. He holds an MA in Art History and an MA in Curatorial Studies from Sorbonne Université as well as an MA in Fine Arts from Beaux-Arts de Paris. He has been awarded several scholarships including René Lassere Scholarship, French-German Youth Office Scholarship and Maurice Colin-Lefranc Award.
